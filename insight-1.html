Why You Keep Repeating the Same Pattern Even When You Know Better

Knowing is not always enough. Discover the gap between understanding a pattern and actually changing it.

You know the pattern. You know what triggers it. You know what you should do differently.

And yet, somehow, you find yourself doing the same thing again.

This can feel incredibly frustrating.

You may start wondering: "Why do I keep doing this when I already understand it?"

Because understanding a pattern and changing a pattern are not the same thing.

Your mind can recognize the pattern before your body knows how to respond differently.

The old reaction is familiar. The new response is not.

So when the moment arrives, your system often returns to what it already knows.

This is why more information isn't always the answer.

You don't necessarily need to understand yourself more. You may need to create a new experience of responding differently.

First, you notice the pattern. Then, you create space. Then, you have the opportunity to choose.

That space is where change begins.

You cannot always control your first reaction.

But you can learn to create a different next step.

This is one of the foundations of The I Love Method™.

Train the feeling. Everything else follows.
