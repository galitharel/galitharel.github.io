The Story Behind The Method

I didn't create The I Love Method™ because I had never struggled.
I created it because I had done the work.

The Search

I spent years looking for lasting change.

Meditation. Personal development. Inner work. Vipassana retreats.

Learning. Practicing. Trying to become better.

And it worked. I was changing.

But something still didn't make sense.

The Gap

I knew what I wanted to become.

I could understand the concepts. I could repeat the affirmations. I could do the practices.

And still… something inside me resisted.

I couldn't understand why.

The Discovery

Until I realized I was trying to create change from the wrong place.

I was trying to convince myself to become someone new. But a part of me still didn't feel ready to live it.

The problem wasn't that I needed more pressure.

I was missing the foundation.

The Shift

The moment I understood, everything changed.

I stopped fighting the part of me that resisted. I started listening.

I learned how to create safety around change. I learned how to work with my mind, body, and emotions together.

And the process became different. Not because I tried harder.

Because I finally understood how to work with myself.

The Method

The I Love Method™ was born from that discovery.

It is not a theory I created from a distance. It is a framework built from lived experience, observation, practice, and years of searching for what actually creates lasting change.

I created the method I needed when I was trying to change.
