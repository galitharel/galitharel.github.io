The Difference Between Knowing and Becoming

Understanding who you want to become is only the beginning.

You can know exactly who you want to become. You can create the vision. Write the goals. Repeat the affirmations. Understand the mindset.

And still feel like you are not quite living it.

Because knowing who you want to become is not the same as feeling familiar with being that person.

A new identity can feel exciting. But it can also feel unfamiliar.

And when something feels unfamiliar, old patterns often feel easier to return to.

Becoming happens through experience.

You notice. You practice. You feel. You choose. You return.

Again and again. Until the new way of being becomes less foreign.

You don't become someone new by thinking about them once.

You become familiar with being them.

Train the feeling.
