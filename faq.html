FAQ

Questions are part of the process.

Here are the answers to the questions people ask most often about The I Love Method™.

The I Love Method™ is a framework for creating lasting change by working with your mind, body, and emotions together. It helps you understand your patterns, work with resistance, create emotional safety around change, and train the feeling of who you are becoming.
The I Love Method™ is not a traditional nervous system regulation course. It provides a foundation that helps nervous system work, manifestation, and personal growth work together. The method focuses on how awareness, emotional safety, and the feeling of a new identity influence lasting change.
Manifestation is one of the areas The I Love Method™ can support. But the method is not about simply thinking positively or repeating affirmations until you believe them. It explores what happens when your mind wants something new but your body is not yet familiar with experiencing it. The goal is to make change feel possible to live — not just possible to imagine.
