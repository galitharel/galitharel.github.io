Resistance Is Information

What your patterns may be trying to show you.

Resistance often sounds like: "I can't." "This isn't me." "I don't believe that." "I'm not ready."

Most of us immediately try to fight it.

We push harder. Repeat the affirmation. Force the positive thought. Try to overpower the resistance.

But resistance may not be proof that you cannot change.

It may be showing you where something still feels unfamiliar.

Instead of asking "How do I get rid of this resistance?" try asking "What is this showing me?"

That question changes the relationship.

You stop treating yourself like a problem to solve. You start listening.

And awareness creates the possibility of choice.

Resistance is not always the wall.

Sometimes it is the information that shows you where the bridge needs to be built.
